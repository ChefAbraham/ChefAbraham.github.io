Information

Difficulty: Easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 50 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour & 0 minutes
Serves: 8
Nutrition Per Serving(Cals/Fat/Protein/Carbs): 343/18g/30g/20g
Total Cost/Cost Per Serving: $31.33/$3.92

Ingredients

1.5 large yellow onion, diced
1 bell pepper, sliced
6 cloves garlic, roughly sliced
2 stalks celery, sliced
2 tbsp. tomato paste
2 lbs. large shrimp, peeled, deveined and tails-off
1 quart chicken, fish, or vegetable stock
1 28 oz. can whole peeled tomatoes
1 tbsp. smoked paprika
1 tbsp. granulated garlic
1-2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tbsp. oregano
1 tbsp. dried basil
2 tbsp. avocado oil
4 tbsp. butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup crema (Mexican table cream)

Equipment

Cutting Board
Knife
Large Pot
Rubber Spatula
Ladle
Mixing Bowl

Instructions

1. Heat 2 tbsp. avocado oil in a large (8 qt.+ pot). Once hot, add 1.5 diced yellow onions, 1 sliced bell pepper, and 6 cloves roughly sliced garlic to oil. Season with salt and pepper, stir well with rubber spatula.

2. Once edges of onions begin to brown, about 10 minutes, add about 2 tbsp. of tomato paste and stir well. Season with 1 tbsp. granualted garlic, 1 tsp. cayenne pepper, 1 tbsp. smoked paprika, 1 tbsp. dried basil & 1 tbsp. dried oregano. Add 4 tbsp. butter and allow to melt completely.

3. Once butter has melted, sprinkle 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour over the top of the vegetable mixture. Stir well. Allow the roux to cook until the edges of the mixture have a slight caramel color, about 3-4 minutes.

4. Pour in 1 quart of chicken, vegetable, or fish stock. Add 1 28 oz. can whole peeled tomatoes. Fill tomato can about 1/2 with water and add to the pot. Add 1 bag of frozen southwestern blend or frozen corn. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer.

5. Allow soup to simmer, uncovered, for at least 25 and up to 50 minutes until it reaches desired thickness. Add 1/2 cup of crema and 2 lbs. large shrimp, peeled, deveined and tails removed. Bring to a rolling simmer and allow to cook for 10 minutes.

6. Serve over a bed of jasmine rice with sliced serrano peppers, sliced green onions, and shredded cheddar cheese as garnish.
